Summary

The minions want to party but Gru has a little stunt for them in exchange of this. He wants his minions to get as many bananas as possible. He has three missions which are full of hurdles, treats and bananas. The minions can pick any mission of choice and collect bananas for the party. More the banana, merrier the partyyy. Wishing our minions luck in this adventurous and fun ride.

The game has below three missions which you can chosse from:

Desert

Space

Water

Key Features

  • Endless Runner: IOS 3D Game
  • Action: Running and Jumping
  • Score Calculation: Bananas and precious items collected
  • Energy: Gained and Reduced based on good and stale bananas collected
  • Lifeline: Gains lifeline on 100% completion of energy
  • Obstacle: Reduces lifeline and eventually ends game
  • Speed: Controlled by energy level and lifeline count
